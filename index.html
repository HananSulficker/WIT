THE PRINCES OF KPOP

BTS debuted on 13th June 2013 under BigHit entertainment with 7 members ranging from different parts of South Korea.Their fandom is known as ARMY. The representative color is purple which stands for love and peace. BTS is the first Kpop group to be nominated for the grammys. The boys endured a lot to have reached the heights they are at now. And true well wishers are extremely proud of what they have achieved. They have taught their fans to love themselves for who they are and to not alter yourself to fit the world standards set by a few.
